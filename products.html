Manual Mobility

Manual Wheelchairs

Our manual wheelchair lineup covers every need — from lightweight transport chairs to heavy-duty bariatric models. All chairs can be custom-fitted to your body dimensions and functional needs by our certified ATP specialists.

Whether you need a chair for everyday independence or post-surgical recovery, we'll match you with the right model and ensure proper fit to prevent pressure injuries and maximize comfort.

Standard & transport chairs
Ultra-lightweight & titanium
Heavy-duty & bariatric
Custom seating & cushions
Folding & rigid frames
Footrest & armrest options
Medicare Part B covers manual wheelchairs as Durable Medical Equipment with physician documentation

Power Mobility

Power Wheelchairs

Power wheelchairs provide maximum independence for patients with limited upper body strength or endurance. Our lineup includes Group 2 standard power chairs through Group 5 complex rehab technology (CRT) — the most advanced power mobility available.

Every power chair is programmed and fitted by our ATP-certified specialists using clinical assessment protocols to ensure the safest, most functional outcome for each patient.

Group 2, 3, 4, 5 chairs
Front, mid & rear wheel drive
Tilt-in-space systems
Power recline & elevating legrests
Alternate drive controls
Up to 20-mile battery range
Medicare Group 2–5 coverage available with physician order and face-to-face examination

Mobility Scooters

Mobility scooters offer an ideal solution for patients who can walk short distances but need assistance for longer trips. Our 3-wheel and 4-wheel scooters are available in travel, standard, and heavy-duty configurations.
