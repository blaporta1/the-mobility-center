Simple Process

Getting Your Equipment is Easy

We handle the paperwork, insurance billing, and delivery — so you can focus on what matters.

1

Contact Us

Call, email, or fill out our online form. Tell us about your mobility needs and insurance information.

2

Insurance Verification

We verify your coverage and benefits with Medicare, Medicaid, or your private insurer — at no cost to you.

3

Doctor's Order

We coordinate with your physician to obtain the required prescription and clinical documentation.

4

Delivery & Fitting

A trained specialist delivers and fits your equipment at home, providing full setup and usage training.
